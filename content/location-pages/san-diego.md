## Why Custom Over Stock or Semi-Custom

The cabinetry market offers three broad tiers: stock, semi-custom, and full custom. Understanding the differences helps San Diego homeowners make a decision that aligns with their expectations and the value of their property.

**Stock cabinetry** is mass-produced in predetermined sizes, styles, and finishes. It is the most affordable option but offers the least flexibility. Cabinets are available only in standard increments -- typically three-inch width intervals -- which means filler strips and dead space are inevitable in anything other than a perfectly standard room. Finish and material options are limited, interior configurations are basic, and construction quality varies widely. For a luxury San Diego home, stock cabinetry rarely meets the mark.

**Semi-custom cabinetry** provides more finish, style, and sizing options than stock, but still operates within a manufacturer's predefined range. You can modify certain dimensions and choose from an expanded palette of doors and finishes, but you are ultimately working within the boundaries of what the factory offers. Semi-custom can be appropriate for some projects, but it still requires compromises in homes with non-standard dimensions or highly specific design goals.

**Full custom cabinetry** -- the level at which Inplace Studio operates -- imposes no constraints on size, material, finish, or configuration. Every component is built from scratch to your specifications. This means cabinets that fit your walls perfectly, drawer interiors configured to hold exactly what you need, and finish combinations that are unavailable in any catalog. The construction methods used by our partners -- dovetail joinery, solid wood drawer boxes, premium soft-close hardware, and furniture-grade finishing processes -- result in cabinetry that performs flawlessly and retains its beauty for decades.

For San Diego homeowners who view their kitchen as both a functional workspace and a defining feature of their home, full custom is not an extravagance. It is the only approach that delivers a result worthy of the investment.
