## 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide functionality, analyze usage, and support advertising. Below is a summary of the technologies we use.

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for our website to function properly. They include cookies set by Cloudflare for security and performance, and cookies required by WordPress for basic site operation.

### Analytics Cookies

- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** -- Collects anonymized data about how visitors use our website, including pages viewed, session duration, and user flow. GA4 uses first-party cookies and does not store IP addresses in full.
- **Microsoft Clarity** -- Records anonymized visitor sessions, heatmaps, and interaction data to help us understand how users navigate our site. Clarity masks sensitive input fields by default.

### Advertising and Remarketing Cookies

- **Google Ads** -- Places cookies to track conversions (such as form submissions after clicking an ad) and to build remarketing audiences so we can show relevant ads to past visitors on Google's network.
- **Meta (Facebook) Pixel** -- Tracks conversions and builds audiences for remarketing on Facebook and Instagram. Collects data such as pages viewed and actions taken on our site.

### Tag Management

- **Google Tag Manager** -- Manages the deployment of the analytics and advertising tags listed above. Google Tag Manager itself does not collect personal data but facilitates the loading of other tags that may.

### Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View what cookies are set on your device
- Block all or specific cookies
- Delete existing cookies
- Set preferences for certain websites

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

You can also opt out of specific tracking services:

- **Google Analytics opt-out:** Install the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Google Ads personalization:** Visit [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- **Meta/Facebook ad preferences:** Visit [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences)
- **General opt-out for interest-based advertising:** Visit the [Digital Advertising Alliance](https://optout.aboutads.info/) or [Network Advertising Initiative](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)

## 9. Your Rights Under the CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

As a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the CCPA and its amendment, the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

### Right to Know
You have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, the business purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.

### Right to Delete
You have the right to request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (such as completing a transaction, detecting security incidents, or complying with legal obligations).

### Right to Correct
You have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

### Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing
While we do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense, certain uses of tracking technologies (such as remarketing pixels) may constitute "sharing" under the CCPA. You have the right to opt out of this sharing. You can do so by:

- Adjusting your cookie preferences on our website
- Using the opt-out links provided in Section 5
- Contacting us directly (see Section 15)

### Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information
If we collect sensitive personal information, you have the right to limit its use to what is necessary to provide our services.

### Right to Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. You will not receive different pricing, a different quality of service, or be denied service because you exercised a privacy right.

### How to Exercise Your Rights
To submit a CCPA request, you may:

- **Email us** at [EMAIL]
- **Call us** at [PHONE]
- **Write to us** at [BUSINESS ADDRESS]

We will verify your identity before processing your request. We aim to respond to all verified requests within 45 days. If we need additional time, we will notify you of the extension and the reason for it.

You may also designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. We may require proof of authorization and verify your identity directly.

## 10. Your Rights Under the GDPR (If Applicable)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

- **Right of access** -- to obtain a copy of your personal data
- **Right to rectification** -- to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to erasure** -- to request deletion of your data under certain conditions
- **Right to restrict processing** -- to limit how we use your data
- **Right to data portability** -- to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Right to object** -- to object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
- **Right to withdraw consent** -- where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

Our lawful bases for processing your data include your consent, the performance of a contract (e.g., providing design services), and our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving our website).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 15.
