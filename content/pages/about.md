## Our Story

Inplace Studio was born from a simple conviction: every homeowner deserves a kitchen that feels unmistakably theirs. Not pulled from a catalog. Not assembled from stock parts. Designed from the ground up, built to order, and crafted to last a lifetime.

We founded this studio because we saw a gap in Southern California. Homeowners with extraordinary taste were settling for ordinary kitchens. Cookie-cutter layouts. Mass-produced cabinetry. Designs that belonged to no one in particular. We believed there was a better way — and we built a studio around it.

Our philosophy is straightforward. Your kitchen should be a reflection of how you live, how you cook, how you gather. Every line, every material, every detail should feel intentional. That is why we never work from templates. Every project begins with a conversation, and every design is born from that dialogue. No two Inplace kitchens are alike, because no two homeowners are alike.

This commitment extends to the way we build. We carry no inventory. Every cabinet, every panel, every finish is made to order — specified for your space and your vision alone. We partner with the finest suppliers in the world: SieMatic, Woodmode, Renzo Restelli, Signature Custom Cabinetry, and locally produced full-custom lines. These are not names we drop. They are relationships we have cultivated over years, giving our clients access to materials and craftsmanship that most studios simply cannot offer.

What truly sets Inplace apart is our scale. We are a boutique studio, not a factory showroom. When you work with us, you work with the people who own this business, who design your kitchen, who manage your project from first sketch to final installation. There is no handoff to a call center. No layers between you and the people doing the work. Just a small, dedicated team that treats your home the way we would treat our own.

## Our Values

### Craftsmanship

We believe a kitchen should be built the way fine furniture is built — with intention, skill, and pride. Every joint, every finish, every detail matters. Our suppliers and craftspeople share this belief, and it shows in every project we deliver. Cutting corners is not in our vocabulary.

### Collaboration

Great kitchens are not designed in isolation. They are designed in partnership — between our team and you. We listen before we sketch. We refine before we build. Your input is not a courtesy; it is the foundation of every decision we make.

### Excellence

We hold ourselves to a standard that goes beyond industry norms. From the precision of our estimates to the quality of our installations, excellence is not an aspiration. It is a requirement. Every project that leaves our hands carries our name, and we take that seriously.
