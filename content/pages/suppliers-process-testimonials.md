## Hero Section

### Our Partners

Every extraordinary kitchen begins with extraordinary materials. At Inplace Studio, we have cultivated relationships with the world's most respected cabinetry manufacturers -- brands whose commitment to quality, innovation, and timeless design mirrors our own. Each supplier in our portfolio has been selected for the distinct character they bring to our clients' homes.

---

## SieMatic

### German Precision, Timeless Elegance

Since 1929, SieMatic has defined what it means to bring architectural precision to the kitchen. Their handleless systems, meticulous engineering, and understated refinement have made them a benchmark for luxury kitchens worldwide. Inplace Studio partners with SieMatic because their design philosophy aligns with our belief that a kitchen should feel both effortless and enduring. When you choose SieMatic through our studio, you gain access to a range of finishes, configurations, and spatial concepts that transform cooking spaces into quiet masterpieces of modern living.

---

## Woodmode

### American Craftsmanship, Limitless Possibility

Founded in 1942, Woodmode has spent over eight decades perfecting the art of fully custom American cabinetry. Every piece is built to your exact specifications -- no standard sizing, no compromises -- with an extensive finish library that spans hundreds of options from hand-rubbed lacquers to richly stained hardwoods. We recommend Woodmode for clients who want the warmth and depth of handcrafted woodwork combined with a level of customization that is genuinely without limits. Their heritage of quality ensures that each cabinet is as structurally sound as it is beautiful.

---

## Renzo Restelli

### Italian Innovation, Contemporary Soul

Renzo Restelli brings the best of Italian design thinking to the modern kitchen -- clean lines, bold material choices, and an unmistakable sense of sophistication. Their cabinetry is defined by innovative use of glass, metal, and lacquer, creating spaces that feel both sculptural and deeply functional. We partner with Renzo Restelli for clients drawn to a European aesthetic that pushes boundaries while remaining perfectly livable. The result is a kitchen that feels like it belongs in a design gallery and in your everyday life simultaneously.

---

## Signature Custom Cabinetry

### Artisan Quality, Versatile Style

Signature Custom Cabinetry offers the rare combination of artisan-level craftsmanship and remarkable range. Whether your vision leans toward a classic shaker profile, a transitional aesthetic, or a thoroughly modern flat-panel design, Signature builds each piece with the same meticulous attention to joinery, finish, and proportion. We value this partnership because it allows us to serve clients across the full style spectrum without ever sacrificing build quality. Their flexibility makes them an ideal choice for homeowners who want something distinctly personal without the extended lead times of overseas manufacturing.

---

## Locally Produced Full Custom

### Bespoke Solutions from Southern California's Finest Artisans

Some projects call for something that no catalog can offer -- an unconventional dimension, a material pairing that has never been tried, a shape that exists only in your imagination. For these moments, we work with a carefully vetted network of local craftsmen right here in Southern California who specialize in fully bespoke cabinetry and millwork. This partnership allows us to deliver designs that would be impossible through even the most accommodating major manufacturer. It also means shorter supply chains, closer collaboration during the build process, and the satisfaction of supporting the exceptional artisan community in our own region.

# PAGE 2: PROCESS (/process/)

---

## Hero Section

### From Vision to Reality

A kitchen of this caliber is not purchased off a shelf. It is imagined, refined, and brought to life through a deliberate process that honors both your vision and the craft required to execute it. Here is how we work together, from the first conversation to the moment you run your hand across the finished countertop.

---

## Step 1: Free Consultation

### Tell Us What You're Dreaming Of

Every project begins with a conversation. We invite you to visit our showroom for a complimentary consultation where we will explore your vision, discuss the way you live and cook, and begin to understand the aesthetic and functional goals that matter most to you. Bring whatever inspires you -- photos from magazines or Pinterest, sketches on a napkin, a list of everything you wish your current kitchen could do. We will also discuss practical considerations including your budget range, preferred timeline, and any structural realities of your existing space. This meeting typically lasts about an hour to ninety minutes, and you will leave with a clear sense of the possibilities ahead and the supplier direction that best suits your project.

---

## Step 2: Site Measurement

### Precision That Makes Everything Possible

Custom cabinetry demands exact measurements, and exact measurements demand a professional visit to your home. Our measurement team will document every dimension, angle, and structural detail of your kitchen space using precision tools and techniques refined over years of high-end residential work. We account for elements that are easy to overlook -- ceiling height variations, plumbing locations, electrical panel clearances, and the subtle irregularities that exist in every home. This visit typically takes one to two hours and forms the foundation upon which every design decision will be built. You are welcome to be present and ask questions throughout the process.

---

## Step 3: Design & Selection

### Where Your Kitchen Takes Shape

With precise measurements in hand, our design team creates detailed 3D renderings that allow you to see your new kitchen before a single piece of material is cut. This is the most collaborative phase of the project -- you will select finishes, hardware, door styles, and countertop materials, guided by our designers who understand how each choice will interact with the others in your specific space. We present options, explain trade-offs, and refine the design through as many iterations as it takes until every detail feels right. There is no rush here. A kitchen you will live with for decades deserves the time it takes to get every line, texture, and proportion exactly where you want it.

---

## Step 4: Proposal & Contract

### Transparent Pricing, No Surprises

Once the design is finalized, we prepare a comprehensive proposal that details every component of your project -- cabinetry, hardware, countertops, installation, and any additional services required. Our pricing is transparent and itemized so you understand exactly what you are investing in and why. We walk through the proposal together, answer every question, and make any final adjustments before moving forward. The contract also outlines a clear payment schedule structured around key milestones in the project, ensuring that your investment is always tied to tangible progress.

---

## Step 5: Manufacturing & Delivery

### Your Kitchen, Being Built

With the contract signed, your order enters production with your selected manufacturer. Lead times vary by supplier -- domestically produced cabinetry may arrive in as few as several weeks, while European manufacturers typically require a longer window to complete fabrication and international shipping. Throughout this phase, Fernando, our project manager, keeps you informed with regular updates on production status, quality checkpoints, and delivery scheduling. We coordinate all logistics so that materials arrive at the right place, at the right time, in perfect condition. Your only job during this phase is to look forward to what is coming.

---

## Step 6: Installation & Walkthrough

### The Moment It All Comes Together

Installation is where craftsmanship meets reality, and we treat it with the same care we give to design. Our expert installation team works methodically through your project, typically completing a full kitchen installation over the course of one to two weeks depending on scope and complexity. Every cabinet is leveled, every panel is aligned, every drawer glides with the quiet precision you expect from cabinetry at this level. Once installation is complete, we conduct a thorough walkthrough with you to review every detail and ensure your absolute satisfaction. And our support does not end at the walkthrough -- we stand behind our work and remain available for any questions, adjustments, or care guidance long after your kitchen is finished.
